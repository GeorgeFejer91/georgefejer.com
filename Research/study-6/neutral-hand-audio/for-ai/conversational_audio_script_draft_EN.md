# Study 6 Neutral Hand Audio - Conversational English Draft

Draft purpose: rewrite the four English audio variants in a more conversational experimenter voice while preserving the current counterbalanced cluster orders and timing skeleton.

This draft does not replace the validated transcripts or MP3 files. It is intended as the wording draft to review before any text-to-speech rendering or duration validation.

Timing rule: the spoken cues should still be followed by protected action windows. The pauses are mostly created by silence between cues, not by speaking pause labels aloud.

Neutrality rule: the wording should stay mechanical and non-expressive. Do not ask participants to relax, express feelings, imagine anything emotional, touch the body, or perform for speed or accuracy.

## Shared Cue Wording

These shared cues are reused across the four variants, with the core movement clusters placed in different orders.

### Setup

Welcome to this part of the session. We would like to ask you to follow a simple hand movement task. Please hold both hands where you can see them, near the center of your view, palms facing down. Keep them still for now.

### A - Apart And Together

Start cue:

Now please move both hands apart and together. Keep the palms facing down. Move outward for three seconds, then back for three seconds, until the hands almost meet. Keep repeating slowly.

Repeat cue:

Keep going with the same pattern. Outward, and back in. Try to make one full outward-and-back movement about every six seconds, until the next cue.

Stop cue:

Finish this cycle. Hands back to center, palms down. Pause.

### B - Palms Up And Down

Start cue:

Let's move on to palm rotations. Keep both hands in the center. Turn the palms up, hold briefly, then turn them down. Repeat at a steady pace.

Repeat cue:

Please continue that same rotation. Palms up, short hold, palms down. Keep both hands centered, and repeat until the next cue.

Stop cue:

Finish this rotation. Return to center, palms down. Pause.

### C - Single-Hand Side Movement

Left-side cue:

Now please move only the left hand. Keep the right hand at the center. Move the left hand to the left side of your visible space, then bring it back. Repeat slowly.

Right-side cue:

Now switch sides. Keep the left hand at the center. Move the right hand to the right side, then bring it back. Repeat the same path slowly.

Stop cue:

Finish this reach. Both hands center, palms down. Pause.

### D - Open And Close

Start cue:

Now let's move to opening and closing. Open both hands wide, then close them into a loose fist. About two seconds open, two seconds closed. Keep repeating.

Repeat cue:

Keep opening and closing. Fingers spread, then close loosely. Stay slow and even, and continue until the next cue.

Stop cue:

Finish this cycle. Hands back to center, palms down. Pause.

### Circle Close

Start cue:

To finish, bring both hands together at the center. Please draw a medium-sized circle in the air. Keep turning in one direction. One circle every five seconds or so. Start now, and continue slowly.

Stop cue:

Finish the circle. Center, palms down. Stop.
