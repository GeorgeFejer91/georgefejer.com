# Research Protocol

## Task Concept

The task is a neutral ambient hand-movement guide. The participant keeps both hands visible and follows slow, simple hand actions for five minutes. The actions are intentionally mechanical and non-symbolic so they can occupy the hands without functioning as emotional expression or emotion regulation.

## Participant-Facing Task

Participants receive one five-minute audio guide in English or German. They follow the spoken cues while seeing their hands from a first-person perspective. The audio tells them when to begin each movement, continue it, switch sides when relevant, return to center, and stop.

The task assumes the participant mainly sees their hands. Wording avoids awkward external descriptions such as holding an arm "at arm's length" and instead refers to visible space, center, palms, fingers, and movement paths.

## Movement Clusters

- `A`: apart and together.
- `B`: palms up and down.
- `C`: single-hand reaches.
- `D`: open and close.
- `CIRCLE`: closing circle with both hands.

Four versions use the same clusters and timing but vary the core order:

- `V01`: A, B, D, C.
- `V02`: B, C, A, D.
- `V03`: D, A, C, B.
- `V04`: C, D, B, A.

## Timing

- Total duration: `300.000000` seconds.
- Setup: `00:00-00:20`, 20 seconds.
- Four core movement clusters: `00:20-04:20`, 60 seconds each.
- Closing circle: `04:20-05:00`, 40 seconds.

Core clusters include direct initiation cues, continuation cues, and stop/transition cues. Cue timing protects action windows so instructions such as "three seconds out, three back" are not immediately interrupted.

## Neutrality Constraints

The task should not ask participants to:

- Produce emotional, expressive, communicative, or symbolic gestures.
- Touch the body for comfort or regulation.
- Evaluate or report emotion during the five-minute block.
- Perform a speeded, competitive, accuracy-scored, or cognitively demanding task.
- Use affective imagery or emotionally loaded interpretation.

## Audio Assignment

Assign one language-matched version per condition or participant according to the Study 6 counterbalancing plan. Version order should be randomized or counterbalanced independently of the particle/environment or hand-mapping manipulation.

## Safety And Secret Handling

The protocol package includes public audio files, source scripts, timing data, and cached prompt clips. It does not include the real ElevenLabs API key. If regeneration is needed, use `ELEVENLABS_API_KEY` or a temporary local key file and delete the temporary file immediately after rendering.
