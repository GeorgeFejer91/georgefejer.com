HAND MOVEMENT CALIBRATION TASK
Version 2   Cluster order: B - C - A - D
Total run time: 5:00
--------------------------------------------------------------
[0:00]  This task runs for about five minutes. Hold both hands in
        front of you at about chest height, roughly an arm's length
        away, palms facing down.
        [pause ~5s]

--- Cluster B: palms up and down ---
[0:20]  With both hands at the center, turn the palms to face up.
        Hold a moment, then turn the palms back down. Up... and down,
        at a steady pace.
[0:40]  Palms up... hold... and palms down.
[1:00]  Keep turning them slowly, up and down.
[1:15]  Now rest both hands at the center, chest height, palms down.

--- Cluster C: single-hand reaches ---
[1:20]  Keep your right hand resting at the center. With your left
        hand, reach out to the left until your arm is almost
        straight, then bring it back to the center. Out to the
        left... and back.
[1:50]  Now rest the left hand, and reach your right hand out to the
        right until your arm is almost straight, then back to the
        center. Out to the right... and back.
[2:15]  Now bring both hands together at the center, chest height,
        palms down.

--- Cluster A: apart and together ---
[2:20]  Keeping the palms down, move both hands apart until they are
        a little wider than your shoulders, then bring them back in
        until they almost touch at the center. About three seconds
        out, three seconds back.
[2:40]  Out until they are wider than your shoulders... and back in
        until they nearly touch.
[3:00]  Keep the same slow, even pace.
[3:15]  Now bring both hands to rest at the center, chest height,
        palms down.

--- Cluster D: open and close ---
[3:20]  With both hands at the center, open them until the fingers
        are spread wide, then close them into a loose fist. About two
        seconds each way.
[3:40]  Open your fingers wide... and close into a loose fist.
[4:00]  Keep opening and closing at the same slow rate.
[4:15]  Now rest both hands at the center, chest height, palms down.

--- Circle close (fixed) ---
[4:20]  With both hands together at the center, draw a circle in the
        air about the size of a steering wheel, turning in one
        direction. Trace the same circle again and again at a slow,
        even pace.
[4:40]  Keep the circle the same size each time around. Now let the
        circle grow smaller and smaller, until your hands return to
        the center and stop, palms facing down, held still.
        [pause ~8s]
[5:00]  That is the end of the task. You can lower your hands.
